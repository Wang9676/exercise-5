Wireframe Analysis:

Squarespace Colima Template

Pencil sketch wireframe:

  • Typefaces: For the main text of information, the designer used (similar to) Devoid and the sizing of them are bold and medium. The navigations type are (similar to) Geliat with the sizing being light. The text are all aligned to the left of the page as the visuals take most of the right portion.
  • Palette: #3C1E16, #151A17, #38383B, #A8A6A1
  • Image: The visuals were the center of attention as they were a wide as the page with the text in the images.
  • Header: The header is in the images and is align to the left.
